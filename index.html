TrueWeatherValues

What is TrueWeatherValues?

TrueWeatherValues is an ideals compass test that is based on the weather community. It is a fork of the SapplyValues and WeatherValues Github repository. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be displayed on a compass.

There are questions in the test.
